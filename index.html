Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Get some peanut butter, jelly and 2 pieces of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread and jelly on the other.
  3. Put the peanut butter and jelly together, with the bread side facing out.
  4. Eat and enjoy!
